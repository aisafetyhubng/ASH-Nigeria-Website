# AI Safety Hub Nigeria
## Building Africa's Pipeline Into Global AI Safety
This is the official website of AI Safety Hub Nigeria (ASH Nigeria) — a nonprofit founded in 2025 to build Nigeria and Africa's pipeline into global AI safety research, governance, and policy.
## What's on the site
- Who we are and why this work matters
- Our five programmes: APEx, University Hub Network, Content, Events, and Global Partnerships
- Our team structure and leadership roles
- How to get involved as a student, university, practitioner, partner, or funder
## Built with
Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build tools. Hosted on GitHub Pages.
